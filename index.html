What is WAWI?

Web Analytics

The science of measuring, collecting, and analyzing web data. It answers the question: “What are users doing?”

Web Intelligence

Goes one step further—using machine learning, graphs, and inference to answer: “Why are they doing it, and what next?”

The Fusion

WAWI blends engineering, statistics, and AI to build systems that observe, learn, and adapt in real time.
